resetToLookAt

open fun resetToLookAt(): ATransformable3D!

Resets the orientation of this ATransformable3D object to look at its look at target and use the current up axis. If this is part of a scene graph, the graph will be notified of the change.

Return
ATransformable3D!: A reference to this ATransformable3D to facilitate chaining.
